Comedy, lifestyle, gift-guide, office/work content, parents-of-toddlers chaos, "stuff I'd send my coworker" energy. We're not picky about follower count — we care that the audience matches the vibe.

Monthly via PayPal or Stripe Connect, after a 30-day return window has closed on each order. We send a statement on the 1st of every month showing what sold and what's coming.

No. The packaging is the joke — we'd rather you film however you'd film any other product. Disclose the partnership per FTC guidelines (we'll send a one-pager) and you're good.
