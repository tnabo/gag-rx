About · Behind the bottles

Candy with a straight face.

We make real candy in absurdly committed pharmaceutical packaging — because the best gag gifts look like they took someone way too seriously.

Why we exist.

Gag gifts had a problem. The bag-with-a-pun was easy to design and even easier to throw away. So we asked the obvious question: what if a novelty candy looked so much like real medicine that the recipient had to read the label twice — and the third read still made them laugh?

Everything you see — the warnings, the nutrition facts, the side-effect callouts, the "America's #1 brand of choice for…" badges — is the product. The candy inside is just the punchline.

What's in the bottle.

Each product contains real, edible candy — cinnamon beans, lemon drops, coated pieces, candy of the season. We source from US confectioners and pack in single-serve bottles and boxes designed to look exactly like what's in your medicine cabinet, minus the regulatory headache.

No actual medication. No active ingredients. No FDA registration. Just sugar with a serious face.

Gag Rx Labs is a brand of V2M LLC. Our products are confectionery, not pharmaceuticals. Nothing here is intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any condition. If a child or adult thinks the packaging is real, please redirect them gently and offer candy.
